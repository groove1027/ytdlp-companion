KNOWN LIMITATIONS:
===================

  • Firefox doesn't support -webkit-text-stroke
    (Mitigation: Use outline property as fallback)

  • Very large text (>72px) may show shadow pixelation
    (Mitigation: Use medium sizes, blur values)

  • Mobile devices with limited CPU may lag with 7+ shadows
    (Mitigation: Reduce shadow layers or enable GPU acceleration)

  • Some Korean fonts may not load on all systems
    (Mitigation: Provide fallback fonts)
